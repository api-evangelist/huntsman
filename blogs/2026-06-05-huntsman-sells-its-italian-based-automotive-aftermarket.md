THE WOODLANDS, Texas , June 5, 2026 /PRNewswire/ -- Huntsman Corporation (NYSE: HUN) announced today that it has sold Huntsman Gomet, a business located in Azeglio, Italy, to Trelleborg Group for €42.5 million (approximately $50 million), subject to customary post-closing adjustments. Gomet is a manufacturer of molded rubber and thermoplastic automotive aftermarket components, which was a business within Huntsman's Polyurethanes division, and was acquired by Huntsman in 2014 as part of the Rockwood acquisition. In 2025, Gomet generated revenues of approximately €24 million.
