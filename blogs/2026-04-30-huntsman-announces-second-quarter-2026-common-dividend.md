THE WOODLANDS, Texas , April 30, 2026 /PRNewswire/ -- Huntsman Corporation (NYSE: HUN) announced today that its Board of Directors has declared a $0.0875 per share cash dividend on its common stock. The dividend is payable on June 30, 2026, to stockholders of record as of June 15, 2026. About Huntsman: Huntsman Corporation is a publicly traded global manufacturer and marketer of diversified chemical products with 2025 revenues of approximately $6 billion from our continuing operations.
